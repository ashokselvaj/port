About Me

Hi, I'm Ashok Selvaraj! a passionate Data Engineer who enjoys learning new technologies and solving problems with logic!

My Journey

2017: Graduated as a Mechanical Engineer from my village, but I was unaware of internships or the job market. I faced challenges, including a job scam, but I learned an important lesson about the importance of real skills and smart job searching.

2018: Moved to Chennai and worked 12-hour shifts in a manufacturing company’s production unit, gaining discipline and work ethic.

2019: Lost my job due to COVID-19, but I didn’t give up—I adapted and kept moving forward.

2020: Took up work as an electrician in my village’s construction industry to support my family.

2021: Led a team of three electricians, managing electrical and plumbing services, improving my leadership and problem-solving skills.

2022: Realized that working in construction didn’t fulfill me. I wanted to contribute to something meaningful, so I started learning Python and exploring the world of data.

2023: Invested my savings in a Big Data & Azure Cloud course to transition into Data Engineering. I believed in myself and took a step forward, trusting that skills would open doors.

2024: Applied to many roles but didn’t get interview calls, so I started learning Machine Learning & AI to enhance my skills.

2025: Actively searching for a Data Engineering role to restart my career. I deeply value this profession—it’s not just a job, but my purpose. I’m looking for an opportunity where I can be loyal, dedicated, and make an impact.

Why Me?

I’ve faced setbacks, but I never stopped learning. My journey has given me resilience, adaptability, and a hunger for growth. I’m looking for a company where I can contribute my skills, learn, and make a real impact.
